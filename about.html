About ACE

Association of Computer Engineers (ACE)
Department of Computer Science & Engineering
SRKR Engineering College

The Association of Computer Engineers (ACE) is the official student-led technical and professional club under the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at SRKR Engineering College, Bhimavaram. ACE stands as a dynamic platform dedicated to nurturing innovation, collaboration, and leadership among students passionate about computing and emerging technologies.

About SRKR Engineering College

Sagi Ramakrishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKR), established in 1980, is one of the premier autonomous institutions in Andhra Pradesh, known for its academic excellence, innovative research, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. The CSE Department is among its most dynamic departments, consistently producing skilled graduates who thrive in industry and academia.
